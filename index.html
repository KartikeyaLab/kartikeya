About Me

A quick glimpse into who I am and what I love doing.

Hello, my name is Kartikeya. I am a dedicated computer programmer with a strong passion for technology and software development. I specialize in front-end web development, working extensively with HTML5, CSS3 (including Flexbox and Grid), JavaScript, and modern frameworks like React.js. I focus on building responsive, interactive, and accessible user interfaces that suit the project’s needs.

In addition to front-end development, I have experience with Python, which I use for scripting, automation, and developing lightweight back-end services. I’ve also worked with C to better understand low-level programming concepts and system architecture.

Outside of software development, I have a background in multimedia production, particularly video editing, where I use DaVinci Resolve to create and edit video content for my projects. I aim to combine my technical skills with creativity to ensure my work is both functional and visually appealing.

I am also a fast typist, with a typing speed of over 100 words per minute, which helps me work efficiently and maintain precision when coding, debugging, and documenting.
