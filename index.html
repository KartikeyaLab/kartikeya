About Me

A quick glimpse into who I am and what I love doing.

My name is Kartikeya, and I am a technology enthusiast and computer programmer with a focus on creating efficient, user-centered digital solutions. I specialize in web development and have strong command over HTML, CSS, JavaScript, React, C, and Python, along with a working knowledge of C++, C#, and Java. My background also includes experience in video editing and graphic design, allowing me to combine technical functionality with visual impact.
